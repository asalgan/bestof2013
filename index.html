Top Ten ... Things ... of 2013

  • A

    Went to San Francisco

    Visited San Francisco with my girlfriend over summer. We rented a car and toured the entire city, Sausalito and Napa Valley. The entire area is beautiful and the city has a very unique vibe unlike any other.

  • M

    Cliffs of Moher, Ireland

    Visited the Cliffs of Moher on the West coast of Ireland. The fog was so bad that you couldn’t see more than 50 feet in front of you, despite the supposedly world-famous view.

  • A

    Joined Starter School

    Going to become a coding guru. Put my startup on hold to learn Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, JS, Git, UI/UX, etc. and build something awesome. Currently in part 2 of 3.

  • M

    SpanDeX.io

    I spent the first half of 2013 working as a project manager for an academic publishing software startup. It was the best work environment I’ve been in and motivated me to come to Starter School.

  • A

    Discovered Rockit Burgers

    Their burgers are amazing, and their truffle fries are ridiculous. Get the Johnny Apple burger with truffle fries, it'll blow your mind. They have a location in Wrigleyville and one on Hubbard.

  • M

    Book of the Fallen

    A fantastic fantasy epic in the modern, “grittier” style of George R.R. Martin or Glen Cook.

  • A

    Started Running More

    I devoted more time to running and pushed myself to go for a run even when I really didn't want to. I've been progressively increasing the distance of my runs and it feels great.

  • M

    The Hobbit: Pt. 2

    I was hesitant about these movies as a trilogy, but Peter Jackson has managed to make the “Hobbit” movies less serious than the original trilogy, while remaining true to the source material.

  • A

    Breaking Bad

    Quite possibly one of the best series on TV, Breaking Bad kept me hooked through all five seasons. The writers and producers did a great job on this show, and I can't wait to see what they'll come out with next.

  • M

    Gnocchi in Florence

    Authentic Florentine gnocchi is the greatest thing I have ever eaten. You are missing out on an integral life experience if you have not eaten this.
